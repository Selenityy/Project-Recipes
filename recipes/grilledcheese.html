Grilled Cheese

The perfect marriage of butter and cheese!

In this recipe, you'll discover a new and deepened love for cheese and bread!

The crisp warm bread, cracks open to a pool of lava goodness. In other words, an elevated grilled cheese.

Ingredients needed

  • Pick our your favorite squishy bread.
  • Only the fanciest of butter
  • You'll want to grab multiple types of cheeses! Seriously, the more the better.

Culinary tools you will need:

  • Butter knife
  • Spatula
  • Flat pan
  • Optional: non-stick spray

Steps

  1. Pre-heat a pan on the stove. Make sure to butter the pan prior or use a non-stick spray.
  2. Take two pieces of bread and generously butter one side of each piece.
  3. Grab your various cheeses and shred them each. I like to mix all the blended cheese together, but that is up to your preference!
  4. Once the pan is hot enough, take one slice of your bread and place it on the pan, butter side down.
  5. Next, put as much cheese as you can fit on the bread within the pan.
  6. Immediately place the other slice of bread on top of the cheese, butter side up.
  7. Wait a few minutes or until the buttered bread on the pan is a nice toasted color, then carefully flip the bread to toast the other side.
  8. Wait another few minutes or until the bread is your preferred toasted color, then remove it from the pan. Turn off the stove.
  9. Lastly, make sure to cut your grilled cheese and take a quick picture of that cheese melting out.
  10. Enjoy!
