PB&J

A childhood classic for all ages!

This recipe will take you back to your middle school years, but without the angst.

Always great for picnics!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly (or your favorite kind of jelly/jam)

Culinary tools you will need:

  • Butter knife

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread and lay them flat next to each other.
  2. Open up your peanut butter and dip your knife into it, make sure you grab a liberal amount.
  3. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  4. Next, wipe the excess peanut butter from the knife back into the peanut butter jar, cleaning it before we dip it into the jelly.
  5. Open up your jelly and dip your knife into it, I would grab a bit less of the jelly than the peanut butter..
  6. Spread the jelly on one piece of bread.
  7. Lastly, connect the two pieces of bread and let the peanut butter and jelly marry.
  8. Slice your PB&J and enjoy!
